Abstract

The rapid growth of IoT devices has revolutionized industries and daily life but has also introduced significant security challenges. This initiative proposes a comprehensive framework to secure IoT ecosystems through robust device authentication, utilizing a secure database with hashed credentials to ensure authorized access. Advanced cryptographic techniques encrypt sensitive data during transmission, preventing interception, while continuous network monitoring detects anomalies and potential cyber threats. By integrating cryptography, secure database management, and network traffic analysis, this multi-layered security approach is scalable and adaptable for applications in smart homes, healthcare, and industrial automation. Enhancing data integrity and privacy, this solution strengthens IoT security and fosters trust in connected technologies, paving the way for future innovations.
