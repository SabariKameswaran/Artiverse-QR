Abstract

Our AI-based spam filtering system enhances an AI-powered video creation platform that transforms textbook content into multimedia formats like videos and music. Using NLP techniques and machine learning, the system filters irrelevant or inappropriate content during creation by applying semantic analysis, dynamic keyword matching, contextual understanding, and sentiment analysis. This ensures high-quality, syllabus-aligned educational content. Built with TensorFlow, Spacy, Hugging Face Transformers, and Flask or FastAPI, the system provides real-time processing and feedback, ensuring scalability, reliability, and efficiency for spam-free, engaging learning material.
