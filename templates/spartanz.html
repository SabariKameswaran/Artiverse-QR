Abstract

A machine learning system can offer personalized healthcare advice by integrating genetic data with lifestyle factors like diet, exercise, sleep, and environment. It identifies patterns and predicts risks for conditions like diabetes and heart disease, providing tailored recommendations for lifestyle adjustments. Continuously updating with new research, the system empowers individuals to make informed health decisions while ensuring data privacy. This approach revolutionizes preventive care, promoting proactive well-being.
