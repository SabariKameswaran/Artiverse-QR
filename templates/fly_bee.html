Abstract

Our AI-driven Resume Screening System uses machine learning and NLP to automate resume parsing and candidate-job matching. It extracts key details like skills and experience, aligning them with job requirements for accurate matching. Advanced semantic analysis ensures precise results, while the system ranks candidates based on relevance, allowing recruiters to prioritize the best fits. Customizable algorithms and bias detection promote fair hiring practices, accelerating recruitment, reducing manual effort, and improving decision-making efficiency.
