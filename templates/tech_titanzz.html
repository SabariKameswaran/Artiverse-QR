Abstract

PlantWise AI is a mobile app concept designed to bridge the information gap in medicinal plant identification and usage. Medicinal plants are essential in both traditional and modern medicine, yet accessing accurate details about their benefits, safety, and proper use remains a challenge. Using AI-driven image recognition, the app would identify plants and provide comprehensive insights into their medicinal properties, potential side effects, and safe consumption methods. With offline functionality, it ensures accessibility for underserved populations. Leveraging machine learning, cloud processing, and local storage, **PlantWise AI** aims to deliver precise plant identification and instant access to vital knowledge. While still in the conceptual phase, early testing of similar models has shown great potential, making this innovation a promising tool for enhancing public health, education, and research.
