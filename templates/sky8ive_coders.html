Abstract

Mentaura is an AI-powered chatbot designed to provide compassionate mental health support. Acting as a virtual friend, it offers encouragement, guidance, and sentiment analysis through emoji-based emotion ratings. Mentaura detects emotional patterns, integrates with crisis services and therapists, and supports multilingual accessibility. Gamified features like achievements, daily challenges, and mood tracking keep users engaged. With cutting-edge technology, it delivers an interactive, user-friendly experience, bridging the gap between technology and mental health to foster healing and well-being.
