Abstract

Ulcerative Colitis (UC) is a chronic inflammatory bowel disease causing abdominal pain, diarrhea, and fatigue. Machine learning (ML) enhances UC management by enabling early diagnosis, personalized treatment, and real-time monitoring. Analyzing clinical records, genetic data, and imaging, ML identifies patterns missed by traditional methods, predicts disease progression, and optimizes care. Wearable devices with ML track health metrics for early intervention, while research benefits from insights into genetic and environmental factors. By improving diagnostics and proactive management, ML enhances patient outcomes and reduces healthcare burdens.
